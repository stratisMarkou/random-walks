The conjugate function¶

Conjuugate function: The conjugate function of \(f\) is defined as \($\DeclareMathOperator*{\sup}{sup}f^*(y) = \sup_{x \in \textbf{dom} f}\left(y^\top x - f(x)\right),\)$ and is convex for any \(f\).
