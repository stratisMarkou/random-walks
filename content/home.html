Welcome¶

About¶

Welcome to Random Walks! This is an archive of explorations, in the convex hull of Inference, Probability, Optimisation and Physics. I am putting this together to keep track of the things I study and to share it with the web.

Modes under exploration¶

  • An introduction to basic probabilities, following the book An Introduction to Probability by Grimmett and Welsh.

  • Approximate inference for Gaussian Processes.

  • Stochastic processes and stochastic differential equations.

  • Bayesian Methods for Adaptive Models: A reproduction of David MacKay’s PhD thesis.

  • The Neural Process family: Conditional Neural Processes (CNPs), Neural Processes (NPs) and Attentive Neural Processes (ANPs).

  • Probabilistic methods for Reinforcement Learning, like PILCO.
