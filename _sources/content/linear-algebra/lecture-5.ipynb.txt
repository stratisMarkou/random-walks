    "# Positive-definite and Semidefinite Matrices\n",
    "\n",
    "## Positive-definite matrices\n",
    "\n",
    "Five tantamount criteria:\n",
    "\n",
    "1. Strictly positive eigenvalues $\\lambda > 0$.\n",
    "2. Positive quadratic from $x^\\top S x > 0$.\n",
    "3. All leading determinants are positive.\n",
    "4. All pivots are positive.\n",
    "5. Can be written as $S = A^\\top A$, where the columns of $A$ are independent.\n",
    "\n",
    "## Positive semi-definite matrices\n",
    "\n",
    "1. Non-negative eigenvalues $\\lambda \\geq 0$.\n",
    "2. Non-negative quadratic from $x^\\top S x > 0$.\n",
    "3. Can be written as $S = A^\\top A$."
