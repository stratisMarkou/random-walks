    "- Writing vectors in terms of eigenvalues and eigenvectors helps\n",
    "$$v = c_1 x_1 + ... + c_n x_n,~A^kv = c_1 \\lambda_1^k x_1 + ... + c_n \\lambda_n^k x_n$$\n",
    "\n",
    "- Two matrices $A$ and $B$ are similar iff:$$B = M^{-1}AM$$ Similar matrices have the same eigenvalues, but not necessarily the same eigenvectors:\\begin{align}\n",
    "Bx = \\lambda x \\implies M^{-1}AMx &= \\lambda x\\\\\n",
    "A(Mx) &= \\lambda (Mx)\\\\\n",
    "\\end{align}\n",
    "- $AB$ and $BA$ have the same eigenvalues.\n",
    "- Real symmetric matrices have real eigenvalues and orthogonal eigenvectors.\n",
